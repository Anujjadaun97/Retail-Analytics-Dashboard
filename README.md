# 🛒 Retail Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive **Retail Analytics Dashboard** built using **Power BI** and the **Superstore Sales Dataset**. The objective is to analyze business performance across sales, profitability, products, regions, and customers to uncover actionable insights that can support strategic decision-making.

The dashboard follows a business-oriented approach and provides insights into:

* Sales Performance
* Profitability Analysis
* Product Performance
* Regional Analysis
* Customer Analytics
* RFM Segmentation

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The primary goals of this analysis are:

* Identify key revenue and profit drivers.
* Analyze the impact of discounts on profitability.
* Evaluate product and category performance.
* Understand regional sales and profit distribution.
* Segment customers using RFM Analysis.
* Discover customer retention opportunities.

* Total revenue exceeded **$2.3M**.

* Copiers are the most profitable sub-category.
* Tables generate substantial losses.
* Furniture requires pricing and discount optimization.

### Regional Insights

* West Region consistently outperforms other regions.
* California is the most profitable state.
* Texas significantly impacts overall profitability.

### Customer Insights

* A large proportion of customers are at risk of churn.
* Champions represent a small but valuable customer group.
* Customer retention presents a major opportunity for growth.

# 🚀 Business Recommendations

### Customer Retention

* Launch targeted campaigns for At Risk customers.
* Reward Champions with loyalty programs and exclusive offers.

### Product Strategy

* Increase focus on high-margin products such as Copiers and Accessories.
* Review pricing and discount policies for Tables and Bookcases.

### Regional Strategy

* Expand successful strategies used in the West Region.
* Investigate causes of losses in Texas and Central Region.

### Profitability Improvement

* Monitor high-discount transactions.
* Focus on improving margins in the Furniture category.
